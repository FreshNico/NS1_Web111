Biography

Hello, my name is Nicolas Sandoval and welcome to my About Me page. I am currently a junior student at California State University, Los Angeles.
I am the youngest of 4 in family of 6. I am 22 years old, Mexican American, and a first-generation college student. My parents and siblings
came to the U.S in the 90â€™s. I was the first person in my family to be born in the U.S so I have lived a life completely different than they have.
I grew up in South Central and I learned how it is to struggle and start from the ground up. I lived in a marginalized community where there
was a lot of gun violence and gangs. I joined sports and clubs such as track, soccer, cross country and math clubs in middle school and
high school in order to separate myself from all of this. Despite being in a very toxic and negative environment, I graduated high school
and enrolled into a university. I am grateful to those who have helped me along the way.

I entered higher education in order to pursue a major in Computer Science. Technology is something I immediately knew was going to be big.
The thought of being involved in these types of advancements in our society made it obvious that this is what I wanted to do. I was always a
big gamer and music savvy person. This led to me also creating music and poetry as I grew up. I thought of it as a way to channel my life and
experiences so that others could relate or become knowledgeable about the lives that people like me live.

Now, I want to focus on the life ahead of me and the things I can do to help people. I hope to further my skills and knowledge in my field in order
to create solutions to problems that underrepresented communities, such as mine, go through. At college, I met many great friends and professors
that have helped me hone my skills in all areas of work ethic, from critical soft skills to computer programming. These skills I carry with me everywhere
I go and practice them religiously in order to put into practice what I was taught and to learn from others.

Thank you for visiting my page and reading about my life.
